Bring your own key

No subscription. No markup.

Cloud video generation genuinely costs ~€25–30 of provider cost per finished minute. A hosted service has to mark that up. Videoboom removes the middleman: paste your own OpenRouter / Replicate keys, the app calls those providers directly, and you see exactly what each render costs. Keys are encrypted with your OS keychain and never leave your machine.

How it works

Drop a song. Get a video.

  1. Add your keys. In Settings, paste an OpenRouter key (required) plus a Replicate token (accurate lyric timing) or a Groq key (transcription fallback). Encrypted via your OS keychain.
  2. Drop in a song and optionally build a cast — reusable characters from a photo or a description.
  3. It writes & renders. Story from the lyrics → shot list → identity-preserving keyframes → per-scene clips → beat-cut assemble.
  4. Preview, refine, export. Watch the preview, re-roll any scene, then render the full song to an MP4 (tagged as AI-generated).
